What is this?

This is a modular multiplication circle. It is constructed in the following way:

First, you draw a circle. Then, you pick a number of points n and a multiplier m and place n points points evenly spaced on the perimeter of the circle. Now label them 1 to n. Then, for every point on the circle, multiply its number by your multiplier and draw a line from the original point to the one you just calculated. If the result is greater than the number of points, you simply loop around (44 becomes 4).

This entire thing is inspired by a T-Shirt made by the Vsauce guys and shipped in the Curiosity Box. If you want to get a box you can sign up for it on https://www.curiositybox.com/. But keep in mind that you won't get the one with the T-Shirt with this motive.
